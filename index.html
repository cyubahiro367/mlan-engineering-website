01  /  Introduction

Origins placed firmly in the engineering profession.

MLAN Engineering Limited was incorporated in 2018 in Rwanda. The company practices across multiple disciplines, offering engineering, management and project finance solutions to a broad range of clients in both the public and private sector.

We bring into the construction industry a standard of professionalism, integrity, creativity and precision — with an established track record of quality developments, contemporary architecture and high-end finishes as a trademark of our completed works.
